Our Services

Comprehensive Healthcare Services at Modan Hospital

Cardiology

Our Cardiology department provides top-notch care for all heart-related conditions. Our team is equipped with the latest technology to handle everything from diagnostics to complex surgeries.

Pediatrics

Modan Hospital's Pediatrics department is dedicated to the health and well-being of infants, children, and adolescents. We offer preventive care, early diagnosis, and personalized treatment plans.

Orthopedics

Our Orthopedics department specializes in treating musculoskeletal issues. We provide care for everything from fractures and sports injuries to joint replacements and spine surgeries.

Neurology

The Neurology department at Modan Hospital offers expert care for neurological disorders such as stroke, epilepsy, and Parkinson's disease, utilizing advanced treatment methods.

Oncology

Our Oncology department provides comprehensive cancer care, including early detection, personalized treatment plans, and supportive care to help patients through every stage of their journey.
